Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Premium plans

How do I upgrade to Premium?
+

Simply select your preferred plan above and follow the payment process. You'll be upgraded immediately after your payment is processed. Premium features will be available as soon as your payment is confirmed.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your Premium benefits will continue until the end of your current billing cycle. We don't provide refunds for partial subscription periods.

What's the difference between Premium and Singles plans?
+

Premium focuses on enhancing your pickleball partner matching experience with unlimited swipes, advanced filters, and more visibility. The Singles plan includes all Premium features plus access to our dating-focused features, including a separate dating profile, compatibility matching, and a dedicated singles pool.

Is my payment information secure?
+

Absolutely. We use Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor, to handle all transactions. Your payment information is never stored on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

How do annual subscriptions work?
+

Annual subscriptions are billed once for the entire year, providing you with a 25% discount compared to monthly billing. Your subscription will automatically renew after 12 months unless you cancel before the renewal date.
