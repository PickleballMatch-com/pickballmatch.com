About This Club

Welcome to Jaco Pickleball Club, the premier pickleball destination in Costa Rica! Our club offers world-class facilities for players of all levels, from beginners to advanced competitors.

Founded in 2018, we've grown to become a vibrant community of local and international players. Our mission is to promote pickleball in Costa Rica while providing a fun, inclusive environment for everyone.

We offer regular open play sessions, clinics, private lessons, and tournaments throughout the year. Whether you're a local resident or just visiting Jaco, we welcome you to join us on the courts!
