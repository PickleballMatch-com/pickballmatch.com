Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I connect my DUPR rating to my profile? +
    To connect your DUPR rating, go to your Profile Settings and select "Connect DUPR." You'll be prompted to log in with your DUPR credentials or create a new DUPR account. Once connected, your rating will automatically update whenever it changes on DUPR.
  • How does the matching system work? +
    Our matching system uses your DUPR rating, location, preferences, and playing style to suggest potential partners. You can browse matches, filter by various criteria, and add players to your reach-out list to send messages. The more complete your profile, the better matches you'll receive.
  • What's included in the Premium membership? +
    Premium members get unlimited swipes, advanced filters, profile boost in search results, see who viewed their profile, video messaging, and priority support. Premium is $4.99/month or $49.99/year. The Singles add-on is available for an additional fee.
  • How do I cancel my subscription? +
    You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription > Cancel. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial subscription periods.
  • How can I report inappropriate behavior? +
    If you encounter inappropriate behavior, you can report a user by visiting their profile and clicking the "Report" button, or from within your chat conversation. All reports are kept confidential and reviewed by our safety team within 24 hours.
