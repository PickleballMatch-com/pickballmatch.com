Best drill for improving third shot drops?

JP
JanePickle
Posted 2 hours ago
👁️ 247 views
💬 32 comments

I've been struggling with consistency on my third shot drops, especially when under pressure. What drills have helped you improve this shot?

I've been playing for about a year (DUPR 3.5) and feel like my drive is strong, but my drops are inconsistent. I've tried practicing against a wall but it doesn't seem to translate well to real games.

Any specific drills or practice routines that helped you develop better touch and consistency?

The drill that helped me the most was having someone feed balls from the NVZ while I stand at the baseline. Start slow and focus on just clearing the net with a soft touch. Gradually increase difficulty by having them feed faster and to different positions.

PM
PickleMaster
I found that practicing third shot drops down the line helped me a lot. I would have a partner stand at the kitchen line and I'd try to drop the ball right in front of them. Once you get good at that, start working on cross-court drops.
Like Reply 27 minutes ago
SR
SpinRally 4.5+
Try the 3-2-1 drill! Three players at the baseline, two at NVZ, and one server. The server serves to any baseline player who must hit a third shot drop. The kitchen players try to put away anything that's too high. Rotate positions after 5 serves.
