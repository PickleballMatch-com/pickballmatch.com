About This Tournament

Welcome to the Jaco Beach Pickleball Tournament, the premier pickleball event in Costa Rica! This one-day tournament brings together players of all skill levels for a day of competitive play, social connection, and beach vibes.

The tournament features Men's, Women's, and Mixed Doubles divisions across multiple skill levels (3.0, 3.5, 4.0, 4.5+). Whether you're a casual player looking for some fun competition or a seasoned veteran chasing the prize pool, there's a division for you.

Join us for a full day of pickleball, followed by a beach party and awards ceremony. Food trucks, vendor booths, and equipment demos will be available throughout the day. Don't miss the biggest pickleball gathering in Costa Rica this year!
